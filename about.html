About the Play

The Brothers Lionheart is a poignant and adventurous novel by Astrid Lindgren, telling the tale of two brothers, Karl and Jonathan, who share an unbreakable bond of love and courage. After Jonathan’s untimely death, he embarks on an extraordinary journey to a magical, war-torn land called Nangijala, where he waits for his younger brother. Karl, despite his fears, follows Jonathan’s spirit to Nangijala, where the brothers unite in their mission to overthrow the evil tyrant, Tengil. Their story weaves themes of bravery, sacrifice, and the eternal connection between siblings, with rich elements of fantasy and adventure that will captivate audiences of all ages. The play brings to life this heartwarming and thrilling narrative, showcasing the power of family, hope, and the courage to stand against injustice.

Based on the Classic "The Brothers Lionheart" by Astrid Lindgren
