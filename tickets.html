Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about attending the show. Can't find your answer?
Contact our box office team.

How long is the performance?

The performance lasts about 90 minutes with a short break.

What is your cancellation and refund policy?

You can cancel up to 24 hours in advance for a full refund.

Can I take photos or videos during the show?

Photography is not allowed, but you may take photos after the show.

Is there a dress code?

There is no strict dress code. Casual or smart casual is recommended.

Are there student or group discounts?

Yes, we offer student and group discounts. Contact us for details.
